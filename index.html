CPAP-Friendly Sleep Guide

Sleep Hygiene Tips

  • Maintain consistent sleep and wake times
  • Create a cool, dark, quiet sleep environment
  • Avoid caffeine 6 hours before bedtime
  • Limit screen time 1 hour before sleep

CPAP Mask Fitting

  • Ensure proper seal without over-tightening
  • Clean mask daily with mild soap
  • Replace cushions monthly, mask every 3 months
  • Try different mask styles if current doesn't fit well

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Dry mouth: Use heated humidifier
  • Mask leaks: Adjust straps or try different size
  • Congestion: Nasal saline rinse before bed
  • Feeling claustrophobic: Gradual acclimatization
